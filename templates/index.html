Welcome to the TTYD Randomizer!

Play TTYD like never before! This randomizer adds a unique twist by logically shuffling items game wide. All key items, partners, badges, consumables, and more can be placed anywhere in the game. Chapters can be accessed and completed out of order without progressing the other chapters. Logic is in place to assure that every seed can be beaten without softlocks or use of glitches/tricks.
