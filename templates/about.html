~ About TTYD Randomizer ~

The Paper Mario: The Thousand-Year Door Randomizer brings new life to this beloved GameCube classic. By randomizing item locations, the randomizer creates unique playthroughs where every seed presents new challenges and surprises.

This web-based generator allows you to create randomized seeds directly in your browser, with full customization options and the ability to patch your ROM.

- Comprehensive item randomization with logic-based progression

- Customizable starting stats and quality of life options

- In-browser ROM patching with no server uploads

- Shareable seed URLs for racing and co-op

- Full Archipelago multiworld support
